Pizza

Description of Pizza

Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature.

Ingredients
  • Pizza base
  • Vegetables
  • Sause
  • Cheese
Steps
  1. Apply butter to the pizza base
  2. Apply sause to the pizza base
  3. Add your fav veggies as toppings
  4. Finish it with cheese on top
  5. Microwave it for 15min on 350 F
